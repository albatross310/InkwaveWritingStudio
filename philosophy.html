Philosophy

Writing tools optimise for frictionless output. Inkwave asks whether the friction is the point.

Writing that remembers

Every word you choose leaves a trace. Inkwave makes that trace visible — not as surveillance, but as a mnemonic record of how your thinking moved. The constraints aren't there to slow you down; they're there to make your decisions legible.

Constraints as craft

A randomly-drawn exclusion set changes each session. When a word falls outside it, you pause. You find an alternative, or you own the choice. That small act of deliberation — repeated, accumulated — is what the app is for.

Privacy by construction

Your writing stays in your browser. Nothing is stored on our servers. The provenance record is self-verifying: anyone can check it against the Bitcoin blockchain without contacting Inkwave. We cannot read your work because we never receive it.
